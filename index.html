I am a Machine Learning Ph.D. Candidate at Carnegie Mellon University, where I am advised by Nina Balcan and Steven Wu, and my research focuses on machine learning for decision making. I am fortunate to be supported by the NDSEG Fellowship and selected as a Rising Star in Data Science. I am also the head editor of the ML@CMU blog. Fill out this form if you have a CMU affiliation and are interested in writing a blog post about your research!

In Summer 2023 and Summer 2024 I was a research intern at Microsoft Research with Alex Slivkins. I obtained a Master of Science degree in Machine Learning from Carnegie Mellon in 2022. Before coming to CMU, I graduated summa cum laude from Penn State University with Bachelor of Science degrees in Computer Science and Physics, where I also did research at the Applied Research Laboratory.
