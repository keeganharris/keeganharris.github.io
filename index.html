Hello! I am a second-year Machine Learning Ph.D. Student in the School of Computer Science at Carnegie Mellon University, where I am fortunate to be co-advised by Hoda Heidari and Steven Wu. My research interests generally lie in the areas of algorithmic game theory and online learning. Recently, I've also been interested in the area of learning under the presence of strategic responses.
