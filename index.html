Hello! I am a second-year Machine Learning Ph.D. Student in the School of Computer Science at Carnegie Mellon University. I am fortunate to be advised by Hoda Heidari and Steven Wu, and supported by the NDSEG Fellowship. I am generally interested in research topics at the intersection of machine learning, game theory, and econometrics.

Before coming to CMU, I graduated summa cum laude from Penn State University with Bachelor of Science degrees in Computer Science and Physics.
